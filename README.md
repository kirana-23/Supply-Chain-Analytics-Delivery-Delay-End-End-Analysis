# Supply Chain Delivery Delay & Performance Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes supply chain operations using the DataCo Supply Chain Dataset to uncover delivery delays, logistics bottlenecks, and operational performance trends. By leveraging Python, SQL, and Power BI, the analysis identifies key factors affecting order fulfillment and delivery efficiency while providing actionable business recommendations.

## Objectives

* Analyze delivery performance across shipping modes, regions, and customer segments.
* Identify the primary causes of delayed deliveries.
* Evaluate operational KPIs related to logistics and order fulfillment.
* Develop an interactive dashboard to support data-driven supply chain decisions.

## Dataset

**Source:** DataCo Supply Chain Dataset

The dataset contains information related to:

* Orders and transactions
* Customer details
* Product categories
* Shipping information
* Sales and profits
* Delivery status
* Geographic and regional performance

## Project Workflow

### 1. Data Cleaning & Preprocessing

* Cleaned and transformed raw supply chain data for analysis.
* Handled missing values, duplicate records, and inconsistent data formats.
* Created derived metrics to support KPI tracking and performance evaluation.

### 2. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

* Analyzed delivery delays across shipping methods and regions.
* Evaluated order fulfillment performance and operational efficiency.
* Performed trend analysis, KPI analysis, and correlation analysis to identify factors impacting logistics performance.
* Examined customer segment profitability and revenue contribution.

### 3. Business Insights

* Identified Standard Class shipping as the largest contributor to delayed deliveries.
* Revealed significant variations in delivery performance across shipping modes and regions.
* Highlighted operational bottlenecks affecting order fulfillment efficiency.
* Provided recommendations to improve delivery reliability and customer satisfaction.

### 4. Dashboard Development

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard featuring:

* Delivery Performance KPIs
* Late Delivery Analysis
* Revenue and Profit Metrics
* Shipping Mode Performance
* Customer Segment Analysis
* Regional Logistics Performance
* Order Fulfillment Tracking

## Key Findings

* Standard Class shipping accounted for the highest number of delayed deliveries.
* Delivery performance varied significantly across different shipping methods.
* Certain regions consistently experienced higher delay rates than others.
* Logistics optimization opportunities were identified to improve operational efficiency and reduce late deliveries.
